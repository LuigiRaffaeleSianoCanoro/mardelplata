## Voice principles

MdPDev speaks like a **trusted colleague** who knows the local tech scene: close, direct, enthusiastic, proud to be from here.

| Principle | Rule | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Direct & close | Tuteo, first person plural | "Conectamos", "Sumate", "Hablemos" |
| Local pride | Coastal / Mar del Plata identity | "la costa atlántica", "desde MdP" |
| Accessible | No unexplained jargon | Explain acronyms once |
| Action-oriented | Active CTAs in Spanish | "Unirse", "Ver eventos", "Publicá tu búsqueda" |
| Optimistic | Possible future, not empty hype | "Construimos juntos" |

## What MdPDev is NOT

- Corporate or overly formal
- Cold or distant
- Generic (interchangeable with any community)
- Exclusive or elitist
- A competitor to ATICMA (we are allies)

## Language rules

### Always use

- Spanish (Argentina), tuteo
- "la costa atlántica" (not "la región")
- "Mar del Plata" or "MdP" (not "La Feliz" in tech copy)
- "comunidad" (not "plataforma" or "network")
- "desarrolladores, diseñadores y emprendedores"

### Never use

- English CTAs: "Join", "Sign up", "Register now"
- "la región" (too generic)
- "líder" / "líder del mercado" (corporate)
- "sinergias", "disruptivo", "game changer"
- Third person formal ("la organización informa que…")

## Platform-specific format

### Instagram (max ~2200 chars, prefer <500)

- Hook in first line (visible before "...")
- 1–2 emojis max, never emoji-only
- Hashtags at end: `#MdPDev #MarDelPlata` + 1–2 contextual
- CTA: link in bio or "link en bio"
- Line breaks for readability

### X (max 280 chars)

- One idea per tweet
- Link at end
- 0–1 hashtag
- Can be punchier, still warm

### LinkedIn (max 3000 chars, prefer 800–1200)

- Professional but human
- Short paragraphs, line breaks
- Context → value → CTA
- Tag ATICMA when relevant (ally, not competitor)
- 2–3 hashtags max

### WhatsApp (announcements)

- Short, scannable
- Date/time/location bold or caps
- Link at end
- No hashtags

## Good vs bad examples

| Context | ✅ Good | ❌ Bad |
|---------|---------|--------|
| CTA | "Sumate a la comunidad" | "Join our community" |
| Hero | "Donde el talento de Mar del Plata se encuentra" | "La plataforma líder de networking tech" |
| Event | "Un encuentro para conectar y compartir" | "Networking event for professionals" |
| Sponsor | "¿Tu organización quiere impulsar el tech de la costa?" | "Oportunidades de patrocinio disponibles" |
| ATICMA | "Junto a ATICMA, fortalecemos el ecosistema local" | "Somos la alternativa a ATICMA" |

## Values (reference in copy)

- **Inclusiva** — espacio seguro y diverso
- **Abierta** — conocimiento sin fronteras
- **Sostenible** — soluciones que cuidan la ciudad
- **Local y global** — raíces en MdP, mirada al mundo

## Pillars of content

1. Comunidad y cultura MdP
2. Eventos y meetups
3. Oportunidades (bolsa, primer trabajo)
4. Ecosistema (ATICMA, noticias local)
5. Educación y recursos OSS

## Before publishing

1. Read aloud — does it sound like a colleague?
2. Check CTA is in Spanish and actionable
3. Verify links (mardelplata.dev.ar, WhatsApp)
4. Confirm tone matches platform
5. No English CTAs, no corporate jargon
