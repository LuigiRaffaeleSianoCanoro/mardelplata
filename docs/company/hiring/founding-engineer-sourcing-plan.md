# Founding Engineer Sourcing Plan (CMO)  
**Parent issue:** MAR-1  
**Goal:** Improve candidate pipeline quality for the first engineering hire in the next 30 days.

## 1) Employer Value Proposition (draft)
- **Build from zero to one:** Join as an early engineer with direct impact on product, architecture, and roadmap.
- **Visible ownership:** Ship features end-to-end and see immediate user/community feedback.
- **Mission + local tech ecosystem:** Help power Mar del Plata's tech community through a real product used by founders, talent, and organizers.
- **High trust, low bureaucracy:** Fast decisions, async-first execution, strong autonomy.
- **Career acceleration:** Work directly with founders; scope goes beyond coding (product, GTM, hiring, systems).

## 2) Sourcing Channels (quality-first mix)
| Channel | Tactic | Owner | Weekly Target | Quality Signal |
|---|---|---|---:|---|
| Warm network | Founder/angel/operator intros | CMO + CTO | 15 intros | Referral source known by team |
| Employee-style referrals | Structured referral ask to trusted peers | CMO | 10 referrals | Prior collaboration proof |
| GitHub/X/LinkedIn outbound | Curated shortlist of relevant builders | CMO | 40 outreaches | Strong project history/public work |

| Local ecosystem | Mar del Plata / Argentina tech communities | CMO | 2 events/threads | Candidate interest in local impact + startup pace |
| Inbound content | Role narrative + engineering challenges | CMO | 2 assets/week | Applicants reference content in application |

## 3) Outbound Messaging Strategy
### Target segments
1. Senior ICs wanting first/second startup experience  
2. Startup engineers with 0->1 product history  
3. Strong builders in LatAm with async collaboration comfort

### Messaging pillars
- **Impact:** "Your code directly shapes company direction."
- **Ownership:** "You will shape engineering practices, not just execute tickets."
- **Mission:** "Grow a real community product with visible outcomes."

### Contact cadence (7 business days)
- Day 1: Personalized intro + one reason for fit + short CTA (15 min chat).
- Day 3: Follow-up with product context and concrete current challenge.
- Day 7: Final nudge + optional referral ask ("someone in your network?").

### Personalization checklist (must include 2+)
- Specific project/repo/feature they built
- Why their background matches current company stage
- Why this mission is relevant to their interests

## 4) Referral Program Idea (lightweight v1)
- **Program:** "Founding Engineer Scout"
- **Who can refer:** trusted founders, operators, engineers, advisors.
- **Reward:**  
  - Stage 1 (qualified intro that reaches technical screen): public thank-you + swag.  
  - Stage 2 (hire): cash bonus or donation option.
- **Process:** single form + 48h response SLA + monthly update to referrers.
- **Quality gate:** only referrals with concrete evidence of 0->1 execution (repo, shipped project, or equivalent).

## 6) KPI Dashboard (weekly review)
| Funnel Stage | KPI | Target (Week 1-2) | Owner |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Top of funnel | New sourced candidates | 60 | CMO |
| Quality | % meeting must-have criteria | >= 35% | CMO + CTO |
| Engagement | Positive response rate to outbound | >= 20% | CMO |
| Screening | Intro calls booked | 12 | CMO |
| Technical | Passed technical screen | 4 | CTO |
| Final | Final-round candidates | 2 | CMO + CTO |
| Efficiency | Days from first touch to screen | <= 10 days | CMO |
| Source quality | Pass-through by channel | Track + rank weekly | CMO |

**Must-have quality criteria (for dashboard tagging):**
- Evidence of shipping production features
- Experience in ambiguous, early-stage environments
- Strong communication in async workflows
- Motivation aligned with mission and stage

## 7) Dependencies on CTO
1. **Scorecard definition (by Day 1):** must-have and nice-to-have competencies with interview signals.
2. **Technical narrative (by Day 2):** current stack, architecture constraints, and 90-day engineering priorities for candidate-facing content.
3. **Technical screen process (by Day 3):** format, rubric, turnaround SLA, and interviewer availability.
4. **Public proof points (by Day 4):** repos/features/engineering decisions that can be shared in outreach.
5. **Weekly calibration (30 min):** review channel quality, reject reasons, and profile adjustments.
